Ok, now that we have all the Image stuff out of the way. lets actually get your characters base items/stats setup.
go back to the root of your mod , then go into the mod folder, and open stats.lua

There is no need to open the main.lua file, and imports.lua is for people who know what its for. if you do not need the imports.lua file just ignore it.

There is instructions in the file of what you need to do, even if you have 0 experience with coding it shouldn't be difficult to setup the file.

you can remove the comments if you want when your done. but leaving them there won't harm anything :)
